## Where we ship

**India only** at this time. We're working on international shipping; for now, the storefront serves customers with a delivery address inside India.

## How long it takes

| Destination | Estimated delivery |
|---|---|
| Metro cities (Mumbai, Delhi NCR, Bengaluru, Hyderabad, Chennai, Kolkata, Pune, Ahmedabad) | **3–6 business days** |
| Tier-2 / Tier-3 cities | **5–10 business days** |
| Remote / North-East / Andaman & Nicobar | **7–14 business days** |

Business days are Monday–Friday, excluding Indian public holidays. The clock starts the day **after** your order is confirmed.

### Why fulfillment takes a bit longer than mass-market stores

Each piece in our catalog is fulfilled directly from the brand. When you order, we coordinate with the maker; they ship to us or directly to you. It's a bit slower than a warehouse model but means you get the piece fresh, in current-season condition.

## Order tracking

You'll get an email + SMS the moment your order ships, with a tracking link. Tracking is usually live within **24 hours** of dispatch.

If you don't see tracking after 5 business days from order confirmation, please contact us at [{{ SUPPORT_EMAIL }}](mailto:{{ SUPPORT_EMAIL }}) — we'll chase it down.

## What happens if your order is delayed

We aim to meet the windows above, but occasionally things slip:

- **Out-of-stock at the source brand:** we'll email you within 48 hours of detecting the issue with two options — wait for restock (typical lead time noted in the email) or get a full refund.
- **Courier delays:** if your order is more than **{{ DELAY_TOLERANCE_DAYS }} business days** past the upper estimate above and your tracking shows no movement, email us and we'll investigate. You may request a refund per our [Returns / Refund Policy](/policies/refund-policy).

## Address changes

We can change your delivery address **only if** the order hasn't been handed to the courier yet. Email us at [{{ SUPPORT_EMAIL }}](mailto:{{ SUPPORT_EMAIL }}) with the order number and the corrected address — we'll confirm whether we caught it in time.

## Incorrect or incomplete addresses

If a package is returned to us because the address was wrong / unreachable / refused, we'll contact you to arrange a redelivery (additional shipping fee applies) or process a refund minus the original shipping cost.

## Lost shipments

If tracking shows your package as delivered but you haven't received it:

1. Check with neighbours, building security, and family members at the address.
2. Confirm the delivery address was correct.
3. Contact us within **48 hours** of the "delivered" status update at [{{ SUPPORT_EMAIL }}](mailto:{{ SUPPORT_EMAIL }}).

We'll work with the courier to investigate. If the package is confirmed lost in transit, we'll reship or refund per the [Returns / Refund Policy](/policies/refund-policy).

## Customs and duties

Not applicable — we only ship within India.
