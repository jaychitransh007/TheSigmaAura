## Style that gets you

TheSigmaVibe is a curated multi-brand storefront with an AI stylist built in. Every piece on the site is hand-picked — clean silhouettes, honest fabrics, India-friendly cuts — and our stylist, Vibe, helps you put together outfits that actually feel like you.

We're built for people who want to dress better every day but don't want to spend their weekends scrolling through ten thousand identical product grids. You tell Vibe what you're going for; Vibe styles the look around what you already own and fills the gaps from the store.

## How it works

1. **Browse** the catalog like a normal store, or
2. **Chat with Vibe** at [Style](/apps/vibe/style) — describe an occasion, mood, or piece you want to build around, and Vibe puts together full outfits with try-on previews.
3. **Add to cart, check out, done.** Vibe knows your size, your wardrobe, and what flatters you.

When you order, we coordinate with the brand to get your piece to you fresh — not from a warehouse that's been sitting on inventory for a year. That sometimes adds a day or two to shipping but means you're getting the piece the brand intended you to wear.
